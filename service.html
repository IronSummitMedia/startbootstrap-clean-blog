Nomi Bobi

Launched in 2016, Nomi Bobi is a 501(c)(3) dedicated to promoting mental health awareness through sales of handmade owl decorations. Nomi Bobi has donated all of its proceeds to support mental health organizations and is committed to its advocacy efforts through community engagements.

Code211

In February 2019, Township High School District 211's second annual Code211 was a success. Serving over 100 students across the five schools, the student-run hackathon and workshops event promoted computer science education. The executive board was led by Maegan who coordinated over 20 student representatives and collaborated with high school administrators to make the event a reality.
