Maegan is a current student at Harvard University. In 2016, Maegan founded the mental health nonprofit Nomi Bobi. Through sales of handmade Nomi Owl decorations, she has raised funds to support both the mental health organizations in the US and the artists in Taiwan who made them.

Maegan grew her impact and brought mental health awareness to her high school. Collaborating with high school administrators, she initiated and organized a schoolwide Mental Health Presentation Day event for over 2,700 high school students.

She further contributed to the mental health cause by conducting research. During her time at Texas Tech University as a Clark Scholar, Maegan determined the effects of social context on the behavior of individuals with anxiety. She looks forward to continue advancing her work.
